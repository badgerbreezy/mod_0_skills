class:
employee

attributes:
cooking (boolean)
taking_customer_order (boolean)
tips_earned (float)
current_salary (float)
promotion_amount (float)
employee_name (string)
employee_position (string)

methods:
cook_meal (modifies cooking true or false)
take_customer_order (modifies taking_customer_order true or false)
count_tips_earned (modifies tips_earned attribute)
display_salary (show current salary attribute)
increase_salary (add promotion_amount to current_salary)
add_employee (adds employee_name to employee_name array)
promote_employee (modify employee_position attribute)
